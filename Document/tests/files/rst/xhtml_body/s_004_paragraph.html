Paragraph with inline markup

Paragraphs contain text and may contain inline markup: emphasis, strong emphasis, interpreted text, inline literals, standalone hyperlinks (http://www.python.org), external hyperlinks (Python), internal cross-references (example), substitution references (), and inline internal targets. There are alsoanonomyous hyperlinks, which also may contain more then one word.

Markup is also allwoed at the very beginning of a paragraph.

Paragraphs are separated by blank lines and are left-aligned.
